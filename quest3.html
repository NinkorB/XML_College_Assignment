Summary

HTML provides different types of lists to organize content:

  • Unordered List: Uses bullets to display items.
  • Ordered List: Uses numbers, letters, or Roman numerals for sequential items.
  • Nested List: A list within another list.
  • Definition List: Used for terms and their descriptions.
